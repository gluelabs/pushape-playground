The Project

You can use Pushape Playground to test your Notification Stack. You can easily configure this app to receive your own notification throught the Pushape Backend

Pushape is a Push Notification Backend developed and mantained by Glue Labs. Pushape makes it easy to add push notifications to your ionic and cordova applications
